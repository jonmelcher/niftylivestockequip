Apache Mobile Creep Feeders
Apache Mobile Creep Feeders are portable up to 3 tons and come with 7500 pound capacity axles (3" double wall steel axle and tongue for "t" frame under unit). The tongue is welded to the axle, so it does not pull off of the pan. The self-leveling panels fold up for easy transport, and are taller and much deeper to accommodate today's taller, longer calves.

Features of the Apache Mobile Creep Feeder

• self-agitating, giving better feed flow

• adjustable doors to regulate feed flow

• weather proof feed compartment

• standard feed saver pan on all feeders

• all steel construction, fully primed and painted outside

• available in sizes from 70 to 225 bushel capacity - 150 bushel feeders in stock
