Promold Winter Water box
• No Water to Freeze - No Water to Heat
• For Dug out applications
• Available as a Hybrid (solar & wind) system

Water is pumped by a mechanical timed switch on cow demand, utilizing generated power.

Designed to work on a wet well. One water line draws from the dug out and a return line drains back any water not used.

Standard units come with 2 access ports and 1 switch, units can be easily upgraded. Swing out door provides easy access for maintenance.

Designed for portability. System can be used year round.
