For-Most Calf Table

Invest in a For-Most Calf Table for fast, secure handling of your calves. You'll enjoy the benefits of a self-catching headgate and a full body squeeze that secures the calf firmly as you easily tip the table on its side.

Features:

  • Self-catching, adjustable headgate
     
  • Headgate opens wide for clean exit by calf, reducing risk of injury.
     
  • Full-side friction squeeze holds calf securely until released.
     
  • Squeeze and tip calf in one continuous motion.
     
  • Sliding tailgate remains stationary while table is tipped.
     
  • Three drop-down branding doors operate independently of each other to allow access to any part of the calf while still maintaining a secure hold.
     
  • Heavy-duty all steel construction for long-lasting durability.
     
  • Headgate and tailgate are reversible for left or right side branding.
     
  • Bottom adjusts from 5 1/2" to 9" to hold calves up to 450lbs
